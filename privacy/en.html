08Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the address below. We will respond without undue delay.

  • Access — Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification — Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure — Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction — Request that we temporarily stop processing your data
  • Data Portability — Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection — Object to processing based on legitimate interests

If you are located in South Korea and believe your rights have been violated, you may also file a complaint with the Personal Information Protection Commission (PIPC) at privacy.go.kr.

09Account Deletion

When you request account deletion, your personal data is deleted immediately in principle. In cases where server backup cycles or legal obligations require it, all data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

  • You can delete your account directly from Settings → Account → Delete Account in the app.
  • Data subject to legal retention obligations will be deleted after the required retention period ends.
  • Deleted data cannot be recovered. Please back up any content you wish to keep before deleting.

To delete your account: Settings → Account Management → Delete Account
Or email us at contact@titistudio.kr.

10Children's Privacy

Our Services are not directed to children under the age of 14 (or 13 in jurisdictions where applicable). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under this age.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at contact@titistudio.kr. We will take immediate steps to delete such information.

11Cookies & Tracking

Our website (titistudio.kr) may use cookies or similar technologies to improve user experience. A cookie is a small text file stored in your browser.

  • Session cookies — Used for essential functions such as maintaining your login state.
  • Analytics cookies — May be used to understand aggregate usage patterns. No individuals are identified.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, although some features may not work correctly as a result. Cookies do not apply to our mobile apps.

12Security

We take the security of your personal data seriously and implement the following technical and organizational measures.

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS / TLS)
  • Encrypted storage of passwords (hashing)
  • Principle of least-privilege access, with regular reviews
  • Data Processing Agreements (DPA) with all sub-processors
  • Access logging for systems that handle personal data

As a one-person indie studio, the Privacy Officer handles all of the above responsibilities directly.
