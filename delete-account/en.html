Last updated: July 16, 2026

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your account at any time from Settings > Delete Account within the app.

Information That Will Be Deleted

Deleting your account permanently removes the following data.

  • Account information
  • Email address
  • Reading and reflection records for web novels
  • Emotion analysis results and statistics
  • User settings
  • Any other personal data stored in the app

About Subscriptions

  • Subscriptions purchased through Google Play or the Apple App Store are managed separately by each platform.
  • Deleting your account does not automatically cancel your subscription.
  • To cancel a premium subscription, please cancel it directly from the subscription management menu of the respective store.

Data Retention Policy

  • Your data is deleted immediately upon account deletion request.
  • If certain information must be retained under applicable law, it will be securely retained for the legally required period and then deleted.

Deleted data cannot be recovered. Please back up any important records before deleting your account.

Contact

If you have any questions about account deletion, please reach out to us.
